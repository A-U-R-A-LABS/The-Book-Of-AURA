# Research Strategy

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

Research is the foundation of innovation at AURA.

We conduct research to understand problems deeply, evaluate possible solutions, and transform scientific knowledge into practical products.

Research is not separate from engineering.

Research guides engineering.

Engineering validates research.

---

# Research Philosophy

We believe that innovation begins with curiosity and ends with impact.

A research activity is valuable only when it increases understanding or improves a product.

---

## Knowledge as a Strategic Asset

At AURA, knowledge is one of our most valuable long-term assets.

Every research paper studied,
every experiment conducted,
every engineering lesson learned, and
every product insight contributes to our collective knowledge.

Products evolve.

Technologies change.

Knowledge compounds.

For this reason, AURA documents, preserves, and continuously expands its knowledge to strengthen future products and decisions.


# Research Principles

## 1. Problem-Driven Research

We research real problems.

Not trends.

Not hype.

---

## 2. Evidence-Based Decisions

Important decisions should be supported by evidence whenever possible.

---

## 3. Continuous Learning

Learning never stops.

Every completed project should increase the knowledge of AURA.

---

## 4. Open Knowledge

Knowledge should be documented and shared inside the company.

Knowledge is an asset.

---

## 5. Experiment Before Commitment

Small experiments reduce large mistakes.

Prototype first.

Scale later.
