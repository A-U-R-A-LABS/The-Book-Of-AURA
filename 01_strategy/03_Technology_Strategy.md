# Technology Strategy

## Purpose

Why this strategy exists.

---

## Technology Philosophy

Technology serves the product.

The product serves the mission.

The mission serves people.

---

## Technology Selection Principles

### 1. Mission Before Technology

We choose technologies that help achieve our mission.

---

### 2. Simplicity

Prefer simple solutions over unnecessary complexity.

---

### 3. Maintainability

Every technology should be maintainable for years.

---

### 4. Scalability

Design systems that can grow.

---

### 5. Reliability

Stable technologies are preferred over experimental ones for production systems.

---

### 6. Security by Design

Security is part of architecture, not an afterthought.

---

### 7. Documentation First

Every important technical decision must be documented.

## Decision Framework

Before adopting a technology, ask:

Does it solve a real problem?

Can we maintain it?

Can new engineers learn it?

Will it still be useful in five years?

Does it integrate well with our ecosystem?
